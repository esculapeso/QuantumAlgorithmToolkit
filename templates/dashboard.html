Simulation Analysis Tips

For best results when analyzing quantum simulations:

  • Time crystals typically show up as peaks at rational multiples of the drive frequency
  • Frequency combs appear as equally spaced peaks in the FFT spectrum
  • The Graphene FC circuit type is most likely to produce time crystals
  • Use 8-10 qubits for the best balance between accuracy and performance
